👋 About Me

I am currently an final-year undergraduate student at Hainan University, advised by Prof. Hua Li. And I am also an incoming Master Student (Fall 2026) at East China Normal University, advised by Prof. Jilin Hu My current research focuses on Time Series Analysis and Large Language Models (LLMs), exploring how foundation models can reason over temporal data. Previously, I worked on Computer Vision.

I am broadly interested in building intelligent systems that understand and forecast complex real-world signals, bridging the gap between powerful language models and structured temporal reasoning.
